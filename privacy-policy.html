Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SFA AI Studio collects, processes, and protects personal data in accordance with data protection laws in Turkey (KVKK), the European Union (GDPR), and the United States (CCPA, where applicable).

2. Jurisdiction-Specific Compliance

  • Turkey: Compliance with Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK)
  • EU: Aligned with GDPR principles and legal bases
  • US: Complies with CCPA and similar state-level regulations, where applicable

3. Categories of Data Collected

  • Identity and contact information (e.g., name, phone number, email)
  • Interaction data (e.g., reservations, inquiries, call logs)
  • Usage/system data (e.g., IP addresses, timestamps, device logs)
  • Audio recordings and transcriptions (for agent training and service quality)

4. Purpose and Legal Basis

  • Contractual necessity – to provide requested services
  • Legal obligations – to meet compliance in applicable jurisdictions
  • Legitimate interest – to improve performance and service quality
  • Consent – required for marketing or sharing third-party data

5. Data Transfers and Storage

Data may be securely transferred to cloud infrastructure in the EU or the US. Safeguards include Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), Business Associate Agreements (BAAs), or equivalent KVKK-compliant frameworks.

6. Retention

Data is stored only as long as necessary to fulfill legal or service obligations. Clients may request early deletion in writing via privacy@sfaai.co.

7. Your Rights

  • Turkey (KVKK): Right to be informed, access, correct, erase, object
  • EU (GDPR): Access, rectification, erasure (right to be forgotten), restriction, portability, objection, withdraw consent
  • US (CCPA): Right to know, delete, and opt out of data sale (note: SFA AI Studio does not sell personal data)

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights: privacy@sfaai.co
